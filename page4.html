WHAT DO THE STUDENTS THINK ABOUT THE CLASS

  • "For me drama isn't just all about acting. Its about being up on stage doing our magic by trying out different roles, gaining confidence by performing in front of the whole school, and to have as much fun as possible without forgetting your lines. In drama class I have met a lot of creative people and dreamers who have continued to inspire me. All in all I am grateful to have been part of this team."

    Earleen Phelia
    G12 Drama Student
  • "Drama class is so much fun. We got to play characters that have different personalities and it helps us learn the moral in every performances. It also helps me achieve important life lessons that we might not learn in any other subjects. We learn team work, that can help us later on in life. Gain confidence in stage and off stage. Drama class teaches me the value of friendship and hard work. Mr Hengky also helps us overcome our trouble in the stage and he is a great teacher overall"
    Nessa Fela
    G12 Drama Student
  • "Drama class teaches us the life skills we need for the future, such as being confident in front of an audience, acting and improvising in any situation. It is also especially exciting when doing productions because not only do we learn budgeting, but we also learn how to control the tech backstage. It takes a lot of time to make a drama, but it's worth it."
    Callia Reyner
    G12 Drama Student
